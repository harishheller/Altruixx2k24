PICTORADES
PICTORADES - is a dynamic two-round competition blending creativity and problem-solving. In Round 1, teams guess AI-generated images based on random words in a fast-paced pictionary-style game. The top teams from Round 1 will advance to Round 2, where they solve cryptographic clues in a thrilling treasure hunt across the venue.
PICTORADES
Each team of 2-3 members will participate in a thrilling treasure hunt, with one member required to stay in the room during the challenge. Round One tasks teams with using AI tools to generate images in 6 minutes based on cryptic words, followed by 10 minutes to guess other teams' images, with bonus points for correctly identifying their own. In the Final Round, teams can use any online resources to solve cryptographic puzzles and uncover treasure clues. Strict time limits apply, and rule violations lead to disqualification. The first team to decode all clues and find the treasure wins!
